Word Matching Experiment

Task Reminder

Let's recap the experiment. Your task is to judge descriptors for 5 images.

  1. First, you see a cross-hair at the center of the screen. Focus on the cross and do not move your eyes.
  2. Then, an image flashes on the screen, before it's covered by a mask. Images will be shown for only a very short time (a fraction of a second).
  3. When the mask disappears, a response screen will appear. Activate the task by clicking the center button. Once you click the button, a word will appear that you think either does, or does not, describe part of the image.
  4. Your task is to judge whether the word is related to the image or not and how confident you are. The left half of the responses mean No [ N ] the word does not describe the image. The right half means Yes [ Y ]. The stronger you feel about the word, the higher the number you should choose (Min = 1, Max = 4).
  5. In the example above, the given word [ Anaconda ] is clearly NOT related to the image. The word [ Bull ] clearly is. Other words may be less clear, and will require your best judgement.

When you press the [Space] bar, you will commence the next Block. An image will be presented at the very start, so get ready!
